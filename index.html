About

I'm not a developer who learned about contractors. I'm an ops person who learned to build.

My background is in product ownership and operations strategy. I understand how businesses run — the workflows, the decisions, the places where things break down. I also know how to build the tools that fix them.

I came to the contractor space through a consulting engagement with a specialty painting contractor in Georgia.
